# Feature: Document Management (CMS)

## Overview
A secure Content Management System (CMS) for managing the lifecycle of digital documents, including versioning and workflow integration.

## 1. Storage & Organization
- **Hierarchical Folders**: Multi-tenant folder structures.
- **Version Control**: Track changes and restore previous versions.
- **Metadata Tagging**: Custom fields for enhanced categorization.

## 2. Workflows & Security
- **Approval Workflows**: Integrated with eApprovals for document signing.
- **Access Control**: Fine-grained permissions (View, Edit, Admin).
- **Encryption**: All documents are encrypted at rest.

## 3. Compliance
- **Audit Trails**: Log every access and modification.
- **Retention Policies**: Automated deletion or archiving of old documents.
