# Feature: Financial Management (FMS)

## Overview
The heart of the ERP system, providing a single source of truth for all financial transactions, reporting, and compliance.

## 1. General Ledger (GL)
- **Chart of Accounts**: Multi-tenant, hierarchical COA.
- **Journal Entries**: Automated entries from other modules (Payroll, Sales, Inventory).
- **Fiscal Management**: Period closing and fiscal year transitions.

## 2. Accounts Payable & Receivable (AP/AR)
- **Vendor Management**: Bill processing and payment scheduling.
- **Customer Collections**: Invoice tracking and aging reports.
- **Bank Reconciliation**: Automated matching with bank statements.

## 3. Tax & Compliance
- **Tax Engines**: Support for VAT, GST, and local sales taxes.
- **Regulatory Reporting**: Automated generation of tax returns and financial statements (P&L, Balance Sheet).
