# Feature: eApprovals

## Overview
A centralized workflow engine that manages approval processes for all modules, ensuring compliance and transparency.

## 1. Request Management
- **Forms Request**: Standardized forms for Leave, Overtime, Expense, and Petty Cash.
- **Personal Request**: General-purpose requests for internal workflows.
- **Approval Chains**: Multi-level, dynamic approval routing based on hierarchy or value.

## 2. Approver Interface
- **Review Portal**: Dedicated view for approvers to see pending tasks.
- **Decision Logic**: Approve, Reject, or Send Back with comments.
- **Bulk Actions**: Process multiple requests simultaneously for efficiency.

## 3. Monitoring & Alerts
- **Real-time Notifications**: Alerts via Email, App, and WebSockets.
- **SLA Tracking**: Monitor approval times and flag bottlenecks.
- **Audit Logs**: Full history of who approved what and when.
