# Feature: eDocuments (Explorer)

## Overview
A universal document hub that serves as the "File Explorer" for all company policies, manuals, and system-wide uploads.

## 1. Central Repository
- **Policy Explorer**: Easy access to HR policies, SOPs, and manuals.
- **Aggregated View**: View all documents uploaded across HCM, FMS, and SCM in one place.
- **Document Tags**: Organizational metadata for quick categorization.

## 2. Search & Retrieval
- **Full-Text Search**: Search within document content.
- **Advanced Filters**: Filter by tenant, category, uploader, or date.
- **Secure Links**: Share documents with expiring public or internal links.

## 3. Compliance
- **Acknowledgement**: Track who has read and agreed to specific policies.
- **Version History**: Keep track of policy updates and revisions.
