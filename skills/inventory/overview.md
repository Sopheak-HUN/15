# Feature: Inventory Management (SCM)

## Overview
Comprehensive supply chain management covering warehouse operations, procurement, and stock optimization.

## 1. Warehouse & Stock
- **Multi-Location Tracking**: Real-time stock levels across branches and bins.
- **SKU Management**: Categorization, batching, and serial number tracking.
- **Stock Movements**: GRN, internal transfers, and delivery notes.

## 2. Procurement (P2P)
- **Purchase Requisitions**: Internal requests for goods or services.
- **Purchase Orders (PO)**: Automated generation and vendor approval workflows.
- **Supplier Management**: Performance analytics and lead time tracking.

## 3. Optimization
- **Low Stock Alerts**: Automated notifications for reordering.
- **Inventory Valuation**: Integration with FMS (FIFO/Weighted Average).
- **Cycle Counting**: Regular auditing to ensure data accuracy.
