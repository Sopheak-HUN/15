# Feature: Reporting & Analytics (BI)

## Overview
A Business Intelligence (BI) engine that transforms raw ERP data into actionable insights through interactive dashboards and custom reports.

## 1. Interactive Dashboards
- **Module KPIs**: Real-time views of HR headcount, Sales pipeline, and Financial health.
- **Custom Widgets**: Drag-and-drop dashboard builder for executives.
- **Drill-Down**: Click on chart elements to see underlying transaction data.

## 2. Report Generation
- **Standard Reports**: Built-in P&L, Balance Sheet, Payroll Summary, etc.
- **Custom Report Builder**: User-defined reports with flexible columns and filters.
- **Multi-Format Export**: PDF, Excel, and CSV support.

## 3. Automation & Distribution
- **Scheduled Reports**: Automated delivery to email on a daily/weekly basis.
- **Role-Based Visibility**: Ensure users only see data they are authorized for.
- **Alerts**: Trigger notifications when specific KPIs hit thresholds.
