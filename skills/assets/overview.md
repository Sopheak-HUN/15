# Feature: Fixed Asset Management

## Overview
Monitors the lifecycle of high-value company assets, from acquisition and depreciation to disposal.

## 1. Asset Lifecycle Tracking
- **Registration**: Barcode/QR code generation for physical assets.
- **Location Mapping**: Track where assets are located (Branch/Department).
- **Custodian Tracking**: Log of responsible employees for each asset.

## 2. Financial Management
- **Depreciation Engine**: Support for Straight-line and Declining balance methods.
- **Revaluation**: Adjust asset value based on market appraisals.
- **Disposal & Retirement**: Workflow for selling or scrapping assets.

## 3. Auditing
- **Physical Verification**: Mobile-based auditing using QR scanner.
- **Inventory Reconciliation**: Compare physical counts with ledger records.
