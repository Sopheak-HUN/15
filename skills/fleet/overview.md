# Feature: Fleet Management

## Overview
Tracks and manages company vehicles, maintenance schedules, and logistics operations to optimize costs and efficiency.

## 1. Asset & Vehicle Tracking
- **Vehicle Profiles**: Technical specs, insurance, and registration tracking.
- **GPS Integration**: Real-time location and route monitoring.
- **Assignment Logs**: Record of which employee is using which vehicle.

## 2. Maintenance & Operations
- **Maintenance Scheduling**: Automated alerts for servicing, oil changes, and inspections.
- **Fuel Management**: Track fuel consumption and expenses.
- **Repair History**: Log of all maintenance and accident repairs.

## 3. Analytics
- **Cost per KM**: Insights into fleet efficiency.
- **Trip Optimization**: Route analytics to reduce fuel and time.
